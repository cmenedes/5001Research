INSTRUCTIONS

  • There are 7 boxes, corresponding to 7 game levels.
  • Each box contains two multiple choice questions.
  • You must correctly answer both questions in a box to unlock the next box.
  • Each correct answer is worth 1 point.
  • If you choose an incorrect answer, you will be given chances to try again until you answer correctly.
  • If you answer correctly on the first try, you will receive 2 bonus points, and 1 bonus point on the second try.
  • Unlock and complete all 7 boxes to become a Mini Box Champion!
